    About Us

    The Government Associate College Jalalpur Bhattian was established in 2014 with the goal of providing the highest quality education to students in the region. The college is committed to offering a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in their chosen careers and in life. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in their chosen field, while also fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

    At the Government Associate College Jalalpur Bhattian,

    we believe that education is about more than just earning a degree. We strive to provide our students with a well-rounded education that includes opportunities for leadership, community service, and personal growth. We are proud of our commitment to providing a quality education to our students, and we look forward to helping you achieve your goals.

    The college offers a wide range of academic programs, including science, engineering, arts, and social sciences. Our faculty is composed of highly qualified and dedicated educators who are committed to providing the best education possible to our students. We are also proud of our state-of-the-art facilities, which include modern classrooms, computer labs, science labs, a library, and more.

    Programs We Offer

    "Unlock your potential with our diverse range of programs"

    FSc- Pre-Medical

    FSC Pre Medical is a program of study that prepares students for undergraduate programs in the field of medicine and healthcare. It typically includes courses in biology, chemistry, physics, and other compulsory subjects relevant to the field.

    FSc- Pre-Engineering

    FSC Pre Engineering is a program that prepares students for further studies in engineering fields such as mechanical, electrical, and civil engineering. It covers a range of subjects including mathematics, physics, and chemistry, providing students with a solid foundation for their future studies.

    ICS

    Intermediate in ICS program is designed for students who wish to enhance their knowledge and skills in the field of information and communication technology. It covers a wide range of topics, including programming, networking, and database management, to prepare students for advanced study in IT field.

    F.A

    F.A is the first qualification in Humanities. With a great range of subjects, its widely chosen by students who want career in fine arts, Humanities and social sciences.

    F.A (IT)

    F.A (IT) is the first qualification in Humanities + IT. With a great range of subjects, its widely chosen by students who want career in fine arts, Humanities and social sciences.

    Associate Degree Program

    This program ensures that all students gain knowledge of study subjects and get trained for life. After graduating from College, our students learn skills and aptitude to work in any industry.
